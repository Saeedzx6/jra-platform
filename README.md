# JRA Platform — Jordan Restaurants Association

The official digital platform for the Jordan Restaurants Association (JRA) — connecting members, managing events, training programs, and industry news.

**Live:** https://jra-platform-web-psi.vercel.app/en

---

## About

JRA Platform is a full-stack web application that serves as the central hub for the Jordan Restaurants Association. It provides a bilingual (Arabic/English) experience for restaurant owners and operators across Jordan to connect, register for events, access training programs, and stay updated with industry news.

---

## Features

- **Bilingual** — Full Arabic and English support with RTL layout
- **Member Directory** — Browse and discover JRA member restaurants
- **Events** — View and register for industry events
- **Training Programs** — Access professional training and workshops
- **News & Blog** — Stay updated with JRA announcements and industry news
- **Member Dashboard** — Manage registrations, profile, and notifications
- **Admin Panel** — Full control over members, events, trainings, and posts
- **Secure Authentication** — JWT-based login with role-based access control
